2, 4, 6, 8: Gradients to Appreciate!

Published: April 6, 2025

Flat is fine, but gradients are divine. They bring energy and depth to your store’s design—if you use them well. Here are 4 gradient combinations that don’t suck, plus the exact CSS to drop into your layout.

💜 Indigo Sunset

background: linear-gradient(to right, #6139F5, #FFB900);

🦋 Oceanic Calm

background: linear-gradient(to right, #56CCF2, #2F80ED);

🌅 Cotton Candy

background: linear-gradient(to right, #ff9a9e, #fad0c4);

🕶️ Matte Black Fade

background: linear-gradient(to right, #2c3e50, #000000);

Apply these to hero sections, CTA blocks, buttons, footers, or even product cards if you’re feeling bold. Want a tip? Keep your text light and your padding heavy when working with gradients.
