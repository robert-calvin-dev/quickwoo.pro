Quick Blog: Frequently Asked Questions

Can I publish multiple blog posts at once?

Yes. You can create and publish as many posts as you want in one session. Each one can include its own metadata, categories, and images.

Does this replace the WordPress editor?

No. You can still edit any post normally after it's created. Quick Blog is designed for fast entry, not long-form formatting or Gutenberg block design.

Can I set featured images?

Yes. Just paste in a valid image URL during post creation, and it will be set as the featured image for that entry.

Is Quick Blog available as a standalone plugin?

Not right now. It’s bundled with the full QuickWoo suite to give you a complete content + product management workflow.

Does it include SEO fields?

Yes. You can define the SEO title, meta description, and OG fields for every post before publishing—just like Quick SEO but for content.
