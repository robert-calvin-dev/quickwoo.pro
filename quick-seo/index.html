  4. Step 4: Toggle schema output if needed and hit “Save All.” Your changes are immediately injected into the site head.

Quick SEO: Frequently Asked Questions

Does this replace Yoast or Rank Math?

Not exactly. This plugin works alongside them but focuses specifically on WooCommerce products. It gives you granular control where those plugins tend to ignore or oversimplify product SEO.

Can I bulk-edit SEO titles and descriptions?

Yes. The grid lets you enter or update all key fields in one screen—no page reloading, no digging through individual product editors.

What about schema and OG data?

You can inject schema (Product type) and set Open Graph titles, descriptions, and images for Facebook/X previews—all per product, in bulk.

Where does the SEO data get added?

All SEO fields are injected directly into the product page <head> section when loaded, using WordPress hooks. You don’t need to configure anything else.

Will this work with custom themes or builders?

Yes, as long as your theme follows standard WooCommerce template structure. If it doesn't, SEO will still be output correctly—it just may not preview the same way in-page.

Does this help with product visibility in Google?

Yes—metadata and schema are core to product discoverability. While nothing guarantees rankings, this plugin ensures your products are structured for it properly.
